# Model Limitations

Canonical product page: <https://justlay.me/grey-mirror/model-limitations>

Grey Mirror analyzes relationship-message patterns. It should be used as a structured interpretation aid, not as a definitive statement about a person's inner life.

## What The Tool Cannot Know

Grey Mirror cannot know:

- Private intent.
- Off-platform events.
- Deleted messages.
- In-person tone.
- Medical, legal, or clinical facts.
- Whether someone is being truthful outside the text thread.
- Whether a relationship is safe to continue.

## Sources Of Error

Relationship-message analysis can be wrong or incomplete when:

- The export is partial.
- Participants are mislabeled.
- The relationship route is selected incorrectly.
- A person uses sarcasm, irony, or coded language.
- Important context happened on another app or in person.
- One participant changed phones, accounts, or names.
- The thread includes forwarded text, quoted replies, or mixed conversations.

## Interpretation Guidance

Use Grey Mirror to inspect patterns and questions, not to make irreversible decisions by itself. A strong report should show what it measured, what evidence supports the interpretation, and where confidence is limited.

## Not For Emergency Use

Grey Mirror is not an emergency service. If there is immediate danger, users should contact local emergency services or trusted crisis resources.

## Not A Diagnosis

Grey Mirror does not diagnose mental health conditions, personality disorders, abuse, attachment style, or clinical states. It can surface message patterns that may be worth discussing with a qualified professional or trusted person.
